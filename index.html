Open Media Player

Accessible to all, mainstream, embeddable…

Open Media Player puts accessibility to those with disabilities front and centre. It is also a mainstream audio and video player, that puts the emphasis on ease of use for end-users and authors.

And its open source!

Learn more »

Features

Open Media Player is built on the widely used MediaElement.js library, with custom extensions.

It is embeddable via oEmbed and iframe on lots of platforms, including Drupal, WordPress, and jQuery.

And, it's themable and extendable.
