Open Media Player Accessible to all

Open Media Player is a mainstream audio and video player that puts accessibility front and centre. We put the emphasis on ease of use for end-users and authors.

Oh, and it’s open source too!

Learn more »

Features

Open Media Player is built on the widely used MediaElement.js library, with custom extensions.

It is embeddable via oEmbed and iframe on lots of platforms, including Drupal, WordPress, and jQuery.

And, it's themable and extendable.
